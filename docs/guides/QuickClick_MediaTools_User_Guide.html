User Guide - Traffic Sheet Generator

Welcome! QuickClick MediaTools automatically converts your blocking charts into professional, client-ready traffic sheets. This guide will walk you through the simple process.

Getting Started

1
Launch the Application
Double-click the QuickClick MediaTools icon on your desktop or in your applications folder.
2
First Time Setup
On first launch, you'll be asked to enter your name and role. This helps track usage and is only required once.

Generating a Traffic Sheet

3
Upload Your Blocking Chart
Click "Select Blocking Chart" and choose your Excel file. The application will automatically detect and validate your file.
4
Generate Your Traffic Sheet
Click the "Generate" button and choose where to save your file. The tool will create a professional Excel file with organized tabs.
5
Verify Your Results
Open the generated file and verify that all tactics are present and properly categorized. The tool automatically removes blank rows and unnecessary columns.
Important:
  • This tool is designed to work with the standard Unilever Digital blocking chart template.
  • Please verify that all tactics from your blocking chart are accurately reflected in the generated traffic sheet, as automated processing may require manual review to ensure completeness.
